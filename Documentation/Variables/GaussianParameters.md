## Description

GaussianParameters` is a list of floats or numpy array storing Gaussian parameters, used by `ResolvingGaussianChromatogram` to fit a curve, by `WeightGauss` to refine parameters, and by `r2_Gauss` to evaluate the fit of the model, to a peak.
