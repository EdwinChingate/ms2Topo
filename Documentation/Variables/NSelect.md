## Description

NSelect` is an integer that determines how many individuals or parameters are selected, used by `FitnessSelector`, `Mate_Generations`, `Mate_GenerationsColumns`, `Mate_MutantGenerations`, `FitnessPeakSelector`, `RefinePop_OnePeak`, `FitnessSelectorVector`, `Mate_WildGenerations`, `Mate_FineGenerations`, and `RawGaussSeed` to control the selection of individuals or parameters during different processes.
