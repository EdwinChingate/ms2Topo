## Description

minIntFrac` is a float that sets the minimum intensity fraction threshold used in `IntegrateChromatographicPeak`, `Chrom_ms1Peaks_Summaries`, `Summarize_ms1_ChPeak`, and `PlotChromatogram` to determine the validity of peaks based on their intensity.
