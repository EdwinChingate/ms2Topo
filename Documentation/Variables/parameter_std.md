## Description

parameter_std` is a numerical variable that determines the magnitude of the random mutation of a peak parameter, serving as a scale parameter in the truncated normal distribution in the `MutateParameter` and `MutateParameters` functions, thus playing a crucial role in optimization and parameter space exploration.
