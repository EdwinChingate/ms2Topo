## Description

PeakData_and_Stats` is a list containing peak data and Gaussian fit parameters and spectrum id, resulting from `ms2Peak` and `mzPeak` functions, and is used in `Normal_Fit` for further Gaussian analysis. It plays a key role in characterizing peaks in mass spectra.
