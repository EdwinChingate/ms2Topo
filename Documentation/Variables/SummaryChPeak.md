## Description

SummaryChPeak` is a list or a NumPy array, storing the summary of a single chromatographic peak. It's used in `SummarizeChPeak` to store information such as integral and retention time boundaries, and in `Summarize_ms1_ChPeak` to store retention time, boundaries and integral of an MS1 chromatographic peak.
